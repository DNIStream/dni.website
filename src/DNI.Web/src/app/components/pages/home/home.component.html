DNI Stream is a live knowledge repository for software professionals

We aim to make development more accessible to everyone, lowering the barrier to entry by providing accurate information that benefits our community and the software development industry.

All our content is streamed live so we can respond to our members' questions immediately, giving them the information that they want on demand.

The DNI Stream repository the following branches:

  • Documentation Not Included: An audio-first, 1 hour long live podcast and vodcast that delves into the world of the IT professional - from development languages and frameworks, to professional IT practices and processes. Documentation Not Included is published the day after we stream to all major podcasting platforms. Guests!
  • Development Now Included: An exclusively live-streamed vodcast that delves deep into software development projects. Josey and Chris
  • Discussion Never Ignored: Our Discord server is active with IT professionals from all over the world.
